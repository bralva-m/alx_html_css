The Last Lantern

A Journey Home

The Last Lantern opens on the eve of a small town’s forgotten festival, where one lantern remains lit against the wind. It follows Mira, who returns after years away, carrying a single letter and a suitcase of old regret

As she walks the familiar streets, memories surface—laughter from a childhood bakery, the creak of the bridge at midnight, a friendship that once meant everything. Those echoes push her to ask what she left behind and what she might still save.

The Last Lantern

Nightfall and Resolve

When the lantern’s flame falters, Mira must decide whether to relight it and face a truth she’s been avoiding, or let the darkness make the choice for her. The town’s people, each holding their own small light, reveal pieces of a past that could mend or break her.

This short novel is a quiet look at belonging, courage, and the small rituals that keep us tethered to one another. Read on to follow Mira as she learns that glowing embers can become beacons—if you’re brave enough to hold them.

overview

"The Last Lantern"is a heartfelt story about Mira, a young woman who returns to her hometown after many years away. As the town prepares for its long-forgotten lantern festival, Mira must confront her past, rekindle broken friendships, and find the courage to face the choices that shaped her life. It’s a tale of memory, forgiveness, and the power of one small light to guide someone home
